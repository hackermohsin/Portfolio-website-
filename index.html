Cardiology

Specialized care for heart and vascular diseases.

Our Cardiology department offers a full range of services including cardiac screening, stress tests, and advanced imaging for heart conditions.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis for disorders of the nervous system.

We handle a wide spectrum of neurological conditions including stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis, committed to improving patient quality of life.

Hepatology

Management of diseases affecting the liver and gallbladder.

We provide advanced treatment options for viral hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer, utilizing the latest medical advancements.

Pediatrics

Dedicated to the health of infants, children, and adolescents.

Our Pediatrics department provides services from routine check-ups and vaccinations to specialized care for chronic illnesses.

Eye Care

Comprehensive services to protect and improve your vision.

Our team of ophthalmologists treats conditions such as cataracts, glaucoma, and diabetic retinopathy using state-of-the-art technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions. If you don't see your question answered, please feel free to contact us.

You can book an appointment by using the "Make an Appointment" form on our website or by calling us directly at +1 5589 55488 55. Our team will assist you in scheduling a convenient time with the right specialist.

For your first visit, please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of any current medications you are taking, and any relevant medical records or test results from other physicians.

We accept a wide variety of insurance plans. To confirm if your specific plan is accepted, please call our billing department or check the list of affiliated providers on your insurer's website. We are happy to verify your coverage before your visit.

Our regular clinic hours are Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Our emergency services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Please note that specific department hours may vary.
